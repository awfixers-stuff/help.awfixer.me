# Notice to Users

due to the nature of our services and the need to protect ourselves we have extensive systems in place to ensure that our services are not use to engage in, protect, provide or premote any form of criminal activity. This may involve the collection, storage, retention and possible transmission of data we know to be used for malicious purposes to the relevant authorities and law enforcement entities.

# Types of data that we collect:

via the below mentioned services we collect various types of data and store it for a maximum time period specified per data type.

## Firewall (the Discord Application)

  <Expandable title="Firewall Data Terms and Definitions">
    <h3>Discord User ID</h3>
    <p>Discord User IDs are a series of numbers called a "snowflake" that are between 17-19 digits long and are able to uniquely identify a user, app or bot account on the Discord Platform</p>
    <h3>Discord Server ID</h3>
    <p>Discord Server IDs are between 18 and 20 digits long, also called a "snowflake" and can be used to identify a guild on the Discord platform.</p>
  </Expandable>

  when you interact with the Discord Application firewall, (which is only known on the platform as `Firewall#3958` with the Application ID `1442958428132868148`) you are exposing certian data about your account to our services, this data is used to identify your account in accordance with our policies and provide the services to the owner of the server.

  if you are a server owner and you install the application Firewall (which is only known on the platform as `Firewall#3958` with the Application ID `1442958428132868148`) on your server, you are doing 3 things,

    1. you are agreeing to our terms of use, terms of service, and privacy policy for both yourself and every member of your community - past, present and future.

    2. you are exposing your Discord accounts email, username, user ID and Server ID to our services.

    3. you are granting us a revokable license to the information and data on your server and any service it hosts or represents (this license is revoked 3 days after you remove the firewall application from your Server).

## We store the data collected via the firewall application for the following amounts of time:

### User Data
  - when you interact with the firewall application in a Discord server, knowingly or unknowingly. We retain the above stated information about your account for a period of 30 days unless one of the following occur:
    - you send a link that is flagged as being linked to a known scam/phishing scheme
    - you send a file that is known to contain, or found to contain malware
    - law enforcement reaches out to us and explicitly requests that we retain data pertaining to your account.

### Server Data

<Info>server data is divded into two categories, configurations and generated content</Info>

1. We retain the configurations you set for you community for 3 days after you kick firewall from you community

2. we retain malicious links/files spotted and/or blocked in your community for 30 days to either transmit them to discord, the authorities and/or to improve our services.

  ## Forum Post Data

  we retain forum data for one of two reasons. When the forum post is still in the forum channel that Support Manager is managing we retain a copy of that data so that support manager can better assist people in the future.

  if you opt into using our forum to web archive feature we retain a second copy of said data for long enough to transmit the data to your website, from there the data is relicensed to you and removed from our systems.

  ## Bot Interactions

  when a user interacts with the application we may retain details about the interaction for up to 30 days as a measure to prevent spam and to improve our services. This data is not linked with the user and the user has the right to ask us to remove this data by opening a ticket in our support server.
